About Us

Huynh is a family run restaurant that serves home-made Vietnamese food in a comfortable urban atmosphere. Huynh Restaurant has been in Houston for over 10 years. Our EaDo location, just behind the convention center, is easily accessed from 59 or downtown. Our menu includes fresh spring rolls, rice plates, noodle bowls, stir-frys, hot soups, and other fresh traditional Vietnamese dishes.
Where are we located?
Huynh is located at 912 Saint Emanuel Street, Houston TX 77003, at McKinney Street, just behind the convention center.
What are our hours?
Huynh is open Monday-Thursday from 11 to 9:00;
Friday & Saturday from 11 to 10:00.
Closed on Sunday, and Tuesdays from 3-5.
Kitchen "last call" is 15 minutes prior to closing.
Do we serve alcohol?
Huynh does not serve any alcohol, however we are BYOB so feel free to bring your own beer or wine for only a $2.50 per person.
Do we take reservations?
Yes, Huynh takes reservations for parties of 6 or more. Reserved tables are only held for 10 minutes past the time. We do not seat incomplete parties. For reservations, please call 713-224-8964 (713-22-HUYNH).
Do we have a private room?
Huynh does not have a private room but we do have a semi-private section that can seat up to 40 people.
How do you pronounce Huynh?
Kind of like "win" but start with an H-sound... "Hwin"
Can we accomodate special dietary requests?
Since our food is made fresh when you order it, we can usually accommodate special requests.
Is this the same restaurant as the old Pho Huynh that used to be in Midtown on Milam?
Yep, that was us. We moved to our new, bigger, EaDo location in 2008.
What part of Vietnam is the family from?
Our family lived in Central Vietnam, in the city of Quang Ngai, until 1990.
